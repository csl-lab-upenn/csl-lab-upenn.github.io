Young Min (Jeffrey) Cho is a PhD student in Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania, advised by Lyle Ungar and Sharath Guntuku. His research focuses on applications of large language models, with a particular interest in developing conversational AI systems to support mental health.
