Shubham Innani is a Research Data Analyst in the Division of Computational Pathology at Indiana University School of Medicine, where he develops interpretable deep learning models for whole-slide image analysis in clinical settings. He completed his undergraduate degree at SGGS Institute of Engineering and Technology, Nanded, and was a Visiting Associate at the University of Pennsylvania. His research spans glioma classification, mutation detection, and prognostic stratification, with publications in peer-reviewed journals including Neuro-Oncology and Modern Pathology.
