Harry Wang completed his Master’s degree in Computer and Information Science at the University of Pennsylvania. His research with Professors Sharath Guntuku and Lyle Ungar applied machine learning, NLP, and data science to problems in misinformation and mental health — including COVID-19 misinformation detection, text-based mental health prediction, and subgroup clustering methods. He is now a Machine Learning Engineer at Pinterest.
