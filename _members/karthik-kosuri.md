Karthik Kosuri was a Data Scientist at the Computational Social Listening Lab. He holds an MS in Computer Science from Syracuse University and a Bachelor of Technology from Vellore Institute of Technology, India. His work spans artificial intelligence, computational linguistics, and retrieval-augmented generation systems, including contributions to DARPA's SciFY initiative. He is now a Data Scientist at the Center for Integrative Global Oral Health (CIGOH) at Penn Dental Medicine, where he applies large language models to automate systematic review processes and leads research in AI and computational linguistics.
