# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-microscope" %}Research

We develop NLP and machine learning methods to uncover insights about health attitudes, behaviors, and outcomes — with a focus on health disparities and mental wellbeing. Our work spans social media, clinical language, and mobile sensing across diverse global populations.

{% include section.html %}

## Research Themes
{:.center}

<div class="research-themes">

<div class="theme-card">
  <div class="theme-icon"><i class="fas fa-brain"></i></div>
  <h3>Mental Health & Social Media</h3>
  <p>Using large-scale social media language to detect, monitor, and understand depression, anxiety, loneliness, and other mental health conditions at individual and community levels.</p>
</div>

<div class="theme-card">
  <div class="theme-icon"><i class="fas fa-globe-americas"></i></div>
  <h3>Global Health & Vaccine Acceptance</h3>
  <p>Developing computational models that capture health attitudes across cultures and languages — including vaccine hesitancy, health misinformation, and precision public health messaging.</p>
</div>

<div class="theme-card">
  <div class="theme-icon"><i class="fas fa-balance-scale"></i></div>
  <h3>Health Disparities & Equity</h3>
  <p>Investigating racial, geographic, and socioeconomic disparities in health outcomes and healthcare quality using online reviews, social media, and clinical data.</p>
</div>

<div class="theme-card">
  <div class="theme-icon"><i class="fas fa-shield-alt"></i></div>
  <h3>Firearms Safety & Public Health</h3>
  <p>Characterizing online discourse around firearms, suicide risk, and safety messaging to inform evidence-based public health policy.</p>
</div>

<div class="theme-card">
  <div class="theme-icon"><i class="fas fa-mobile-alt"></i></div>
  <h3>Digital Phenotyping</h3>
  <p>Combining passive mobile sensing, language, and behavioral data to build richer models of individual health trajectories and intervention outcomes.</p>
</div>

<div class="theme-card">
  <div class="theme-icon"><i class="fas fa-robot"></i></div>
  <h3>AI for Health Communication</h3>
  <p>Designing and evaluating LLM-based systems for patient education and health coaching — with a focus on preserving human agency and ensuring equitable outcomes.</p>
</div>

</div>
